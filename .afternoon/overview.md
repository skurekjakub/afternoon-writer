# Story Overview

## Premise

An alternate-perspective retelling of the fall of Lordaeron. Sylvanas Windrunner is investigating anomalies at the Quel'Thalas border — missing Farstrider scouts, dead livestock, villages that stopped sending trade caravans — when her patrols intercept an unauthorized human mage inside the first ward boundary: Jaina Proudmoore, Antonidas's protégée, who sensed leyline corruption from afar and followed it north without Kirin Tor backing or authorization. What begins as a hostile interrogation becomes a continent-spanning competence thriller as the two women uncover the Cult of the Damned, race against the Scourge's timetable, witness Arthas Menethil's descent into madness, track him to Northrend and back — and ultimately assassinate him in the throne room of Lordaeron just as he murders his own father. There are no witnesses to Arthas's crime. Only two foreign women standing over two royal corpses with weapons drawn.
