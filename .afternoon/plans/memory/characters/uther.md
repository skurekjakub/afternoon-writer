# Uther the Lightbringer

## Core read

Uther enters chapter 11 as the one man in Arthas's command space who can identify the prince's flaw without dressing it up. He is not there to win the argument. He is there to tell the truth about the argument they have already lost.

## Chapter 11 function

What makes him useful in continuity is not battlefield spectacle. It is moral and strategic clarity. He hears Sylvanas's briefing, understands at once that the plague is larger than poison, and also understands that Arthas is about to turn the right realization into the wrong kind of speed.

## Current state

He rides with the mobilized column because duty gives him no cleaner option. The warning lands. The prince still rides. Uther goes with him anyway.
