# Millbrook Spur Overseer

The Millbrook Spur overseer exists in continuity as evidence of escalation. He is not a fully dramatized person on the page; he is the man who should have been holding the real books, and whose body instead proves the network is cleaning its logistics chain as carefully as its village fronts.

What matters is the shape of his death. The room is swept. The corpse is arranged. The first look offers ordinary quiet. Only a closer read reveals the same precise necromantic silencing already tied to the broader operation. That makes him less a local casualty than part of a repeated method.
