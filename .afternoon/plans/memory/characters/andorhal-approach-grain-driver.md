# Andorhal Approach Grain Driver

This man matters because he does not try to matter. He is an older grain-road driver with a patched coat, a cartbook, the current bushel rate, and the ordinary basin directions a stranger might need. He looks exactly like the kind of person a road-based investigation should pass without ceremony.

The reason he enters the ledger anyway is the farewell. He closes the exchange with the same phrase already heard from another grain-side contact, which turns him from road texture into evidence of a pattern. Keep him ordinary on the surface. The story has not yet earned anything louder.
