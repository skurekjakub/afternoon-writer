# Unnamed Escort Ranger

The unnamed escort ranger remains anonymous by design. Her continuity function is positional: she fills the spare edge of Sylvanas's formation in chapter 2, then in chapter 3 holds the village mouth during the sweep and leaves north as Neral's second rider when the warning goes out.

Write her as disciplined presence, not latent mystery. She matters because extra saddles matter in a field operation, and because the party has to reduce cleanly from six riders to four named southbound leads.
