# Sylvanas Windrunner

Chapter 14 keeps Sylvanas in the same half-step formation chapter 13 invented, but now the posture becomes an active method of pressure. She rides forward-right, close enough to hear every word and far enough ahead to make the distance feel deliberate. One tightening hand on the reins at Hearthglen, one full turn toward Jaina when the answer finally becomes usable — that is most of the chapter's emotional vocabulary.

What matters here is not reconciliation but disciplined persistence. Sylvanas forces the full road-useful briefing out of Jaina: the Lich King, the Burning Legion, the body-learning mechanism, the ward failure, the likelihood of Kel'Thuzad's return. She also hears the shape of the omission and refuses to pretend otherwise. The trust file remains open. What changes is that she chooses not to let that file stop the mission.

Her political line hardens too. Lor'themar brings formal recall, and Sylvanas answers with movement. If corpses are still standing up inside city walls, then Silvermoon procedure has lost the right to set her road priority. This is not theatrical rebellion. It is the same field logic she has been using since chapter 1, now sharpened by larger stakes.

The chapter ends by giving her what she can actually use: not a clean solution, but an ugly narrow one. Once Jaina names granaries, bakehouses, and holding yards as possible intervention points, Sylvanas immediately recognizes the difference between theory and road answer. That recognition matters. She is still checking Jaina. She is still hurt. But she is also still the woman who hears the useful truth, takes it, and rides harder.
