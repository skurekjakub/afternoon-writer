# Captain Falric

## Core read

Falric arrives as the hinge between warning and command. He is a protocol man, a King's Guard officer built to hold lines, rank, and access in place until someone earns passage.

## Chapter 11 function

The important thing is how quickly he recalibrates. Once Sylvanas phrases the threat in terms his camp is already living with, he stops being obstruction and becomes conduit. After that, he is pure execution: the officer who turns Arthas's certainty into camp movement.

## Current state

By chapter end Falric is fully absorbed into the southern mobilization. He matters because he makes princely intent practical.
