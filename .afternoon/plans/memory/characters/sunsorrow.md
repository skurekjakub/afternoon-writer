# Sunsorrow

## Chapter 1 state

Sunsorrow appears only as a name in banter, but the banter is revealing. Lor'themar uses him as shorthand for the kind of Convocation performance both he and Sylvanas dread: ornate talk, civic dignity, and a talent for turning practical problems into ceremonial ones.

That single reference is enough to place him in the continuity ledger. He belongs to the Silvermoon political sphere, not the road. In Chapter 1 he functions as a symbol of the chamber waiting at the north end of the ride — the world of speeches, lectures, and procedural drag that Sylvanas is trying to postpone for one more hour.
