# Jaina Proudmoore / Tomas Brek

This is a short, efficient working relationship built entirely on competence. Brek does not waste Jaina's time pretending he understands more than he does, and Jaina does not waste his by performing superiority for a man who is already drowning in patients. He gives symptoms, food histories, and room realities. She gives pattern, wards, and a cleaner model of what is happening.

The important thing to preserve is speed. They trust each other only in the narrow professional sense, but that is enough to matter. Chapter 4 shows how quickly Jaina can collaborate when the other person offers useful ground truth instead of resistance.
