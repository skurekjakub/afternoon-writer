# Cyndia / Sylvanas Windrunner

This remains one of the cleanest command relationships in the story. Cyndia knows how Sylvanas works and Sylvanas knows exactly what kind of report she will get back from Cyndia: fast, accurate, and stripped of speculation. Millhaven deepens that trust by making the work investigative rather than purely martial.

What matters here is restraint. Cyndia does not tell Sylvanas the ground proves more than it proves. That honesty is part of why Sylvanas can trust her with the ugliest parts of the read.
