# Neral / Sylvanas Windrunner

Chapter 3 clarifies what kind of trust exists between them. Neral has already been useful as the calm rear rider. Millhaven promotes that usefulness into responsibility. Sylvanas gives her the chapter's most important split assignment without hesitation: warning north and keeping Miri alive.

The relationship is quiet, formal, and efficient. Sylvanas does not praise her effusively; the assignment itself is the praise.
