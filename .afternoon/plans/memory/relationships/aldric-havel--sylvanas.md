# Aldric Havel / Sylvanas Windrunner

## Chapter 8 state
This relationship survives first contact with real pressure. Chapter 7 let them read a district together. Chapter 8 makes them act inside it. Havel gives Sylvanas the lane, the suspect, the doors, and the fallback shelter. Sylvanas gives Havel what local suspicion alone could never supply: decisive capture, decisive tempo, and the ability to turn a district map into a working hunt.

The witness death tests that alignment immediately. Havel does not fall behind the scale of the response once it arrives. Sylvanas does not overrun the city faster than his local knowledge can support. They remain efficient because each man and woman keeps her own side of the city intact.
