# Corin / Sylvanas Windrunner

## Chapter 8 state
Chapter 8 shows Corin what Sylvanas actually is in motion: not merely the severe ranger who asked for the right pages, but the commander who can make a whole lane fold around a snatch and then run a city escape without sounding uncertain once. Seeing that could have turned Corin into dead weight. Instead it clarifies his role.

Sylvanas, for her part, learns that Corin remains useful after the first reveal. He can hold cover, add the right local fragment at the naming beat, and take the stay-behind work without needing his hand held. That is a quiet but real promotion in how she reads him.
