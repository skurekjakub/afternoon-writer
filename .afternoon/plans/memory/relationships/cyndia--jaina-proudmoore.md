# Cyndia & Jaina Proudmoore

This relationship moves from custody to escort without ever becoming friendly. Cyndia is still one of the people physically responsible for keeping Jaina contained, but chapter 2 proves that containment now includes protection.

She returns the staff only on command, rides close enough to intervene if Jaina overcommits, and keeps helping the mage survive the exact moments when Jaina's attention outruns her self-preservation. The emotional tone is wary professionalism, not rapport.
