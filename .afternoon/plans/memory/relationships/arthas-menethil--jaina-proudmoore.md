# Arthas Menethil & Jaina Proudmoore

The important thing about chapter 6 is that Arthas does not dismiss Jaina because he fails to understand her. He dismisses her after understanding. That turns the relationship from old affection under strain into something harsher: two people who can still speak each other's names and still want fundamentally different things from the room.

Write the bond with residue, not sentimentality. The warmth is real. So is the refusal. The scene only works if both stay true at once.
