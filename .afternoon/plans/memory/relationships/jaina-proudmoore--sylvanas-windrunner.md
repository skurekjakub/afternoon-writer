# Jaina Proudmoore & Sylvanas Windrunner

Chapter 7 makes the partnership load-bearing. They do not soften. They separate. Jaina takes the magical lane with Corin, Sylvanas takes the street lane with Havel, and neither woman spends time defending the split once the city proves it necessary.

That is the real advance. Sylvanas no longer treats Jaina as support, and Jaina no longer needs Sylvanas standing beside her to make her half of the case. The trust here is still practical, still cold, and now impossible to deny.

Write them as peers whose respect lives in delegated competence.
