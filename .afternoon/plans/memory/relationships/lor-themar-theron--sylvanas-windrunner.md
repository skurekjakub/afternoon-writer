# Lor'themar Theron & Sylvanas Windrunner

## Overview
This is the chapter's clearest example of working trust. They talk like people who have spent years shaving seconds off each other's decisions. The jokes are real, but so is the function underneath them.

## Current dynamic
Sylvanas wants the road. Lor'themar knows exactly why and lets her get away with it until the messenger makes that impossible. When the split comes, there is no drama about who goes where. They already know. He takes the chamber. She takes the live problem. Their relationship is built to survive that division.
