# Cyndia / Sylvanas Windrunner

This is now one of the cleanest working relationships in the book. Chapter 14 keeps proving that by giving them no drama at all. Cyndia handles the relay stone, the left flank, and the incoming rider. Sylvanas handles the wider road, the recall, and the strategic response. Neither needs explanation from the other beyond the bare minimum.

What changes this chapter is not the trust but the context around it. Jaina and Sylvanas are carrying a silence heavy enough to reshape the whole formation. Cyndia does not try to step into it. Her loyalty to Sylvanas expresses itself as disciplined usefulness instead: hold the line, keep the messages moving, challenge the rider, answer the distance, accept the harder pace.

In that sense, chapter 14 makes the relationship even more important. The cleaner the center of the formation hurts, the more valuable the intact left-hand command rhythm becomes. Cyndia is the reason Sylvanas can keep listening upward and southward at once.

The relationship remains hierarchical, but it is no longer merely command obedience. It is practiced field trust refined down to signal and response.
