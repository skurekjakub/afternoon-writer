# Captain Havel & Corin

Chapter 7 reveals that Havel and Corin have been solving the same problem from different sides of the city. Corin has the paperwork and magical proof. Havel has the worker pattern, the streets, and the district memory.

The relationship matters because each man's evidence redeems the other's. Neither was wrong. Neither was enough alone.

Write them as parallel local allies finally seeing their maps align.
