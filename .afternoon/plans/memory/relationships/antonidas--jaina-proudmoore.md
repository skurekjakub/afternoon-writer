# Antonidas & Jaina Proudmoore

This relationship is present through absence. Antonidas never appears on the page, but chapter 2 finally makes the strain legible: Jaina brought her warning upward, was not believed, left her master only a note, and crossed the border without sanction.

That means Antonidas now matters in two ways at once. He is still the mentor whose authority would legitimize her instantly if he had sent her. He is also the missing permission that leaves her exposed. Future chapters can pull on either side of that tension.
