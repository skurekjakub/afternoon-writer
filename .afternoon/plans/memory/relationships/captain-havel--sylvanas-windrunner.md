# Captain Havel & Sylvanas Windrunner

These two trust pattern before rhetoric. Havel has the district in his bones; Sylvanas has the eye to know instantly that he is not guessing. Their respect forms almost at once because each recognizes the other's kind of competence.

There is no softness in it. They walk lanes, compare maps, and build a target package out of ordinary-looking labor.

Write them as allied hunters who met late and knew the other's worth fast.
