# Antonidas / Jaina Proudmoore

Chapter 14 restores Antonidas and Jaina to live contact, but not to clarity. The call gives him almost all of the truth and leaves the deepest part behind. That is exactly why the exchange hurts. He understands enough to be afraid and not enough to solve what is really wrong with her.

The relationship's central tension now is not belief. Antonidas believes her. He always has, when it counts. The tension is reach. He can ask for the fuller version, ask whether the plague can be stopped, ask her to come home. He cannot make the road release her. Jaina, for her part, cannot give him the room without giving him the part of herself the chapter says she is still carrying in fragments.

His slip into “Child” matters because it strips away office and leaves only the mentor. Her late use of “Antonidas” matters because it shows the same thing from the other side. For a moment the call is not Dalaran and not command. It is simply teacher and student with a kingdom burning between them.

Then the distance reasserts itself. He promises to move what he can. She rides south. The relationship survives, but it cannot close the gap the chapter most cares about.
