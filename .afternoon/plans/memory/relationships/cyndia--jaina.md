# Cyndia & Jaina Proudmoore

## Chapter 1-2 state

This relationship starts as disciplined containment and becomes disciplined escort. Cyndia is still one of the people physically limiting Jaina's freedom in Chapter 2, but the mode has changed. At the post, she confiscates, contains, and escalates. On the road, she warns, positions, and protects. The emotional tone remains controlled either way.

That makes the pair useful for continuity because they show how the story can move someone from prisoner to ally without ever pretending the intermediate stage is friendship. Cyndia does not suddenly trust Jaina. She still calls her Mage, still helps keep her in the formation, still treats her as the kind of person who might do something dangerous the second nobody is watching. But she also helps drop the ward-circle, rides with her, and keeps the treeline covered while Jaina does work that clearly matters. By the end of the chapter, Cyndia is no longer merely the ranger who caught Jaina. She is one of the rangers making sure Jaina survives long enough to be useful.
