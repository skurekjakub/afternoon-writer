# Sylvanas Windrunner / Village Militia Captain

This relationship lasts only a chapter, but it is important because it shows how Sylvanas handles local denial without bullying it into uselessness. The captain is trying to keep his village ordinary. Sylvanas makes ordinary impossible by naming details he cannot stop seeing once she says them aloud. She does not charm him. She corners him with reality and then gives him a job inside it.

Write the dynamic as compressed authority negotiation. He is not weak. She is simply farther down the road than he is, and chapter 4 makes that difference decisive.
