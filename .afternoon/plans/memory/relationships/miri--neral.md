# Miri / Neral

This bond forms almost instantly because Neral understands that authority will fail here if it arrives as force. She meets Miri at eye level, offers water and practical speech, and makes the horse feel like a way out instead of another frightening thing. For Miri, that is enough.

Write this relationship as emergency trust, not sentiment. Neral does not become a surrogate parent in one scene. She becomes the first solid thing a terrified child can lean on. That is why the northbound ride works.
