# Old Grain Seller & Sylvanas Windrunner

This relationship is now almost entirely made of memory. Sylvanas still has no name, no confession, and no clean proof. What she has is a repeated phrase. That matters because it changes the old grain seller from memorable road texture into part of a pattern.

Write the tension here as irritation sharpened into discipline. Sylvanas does not chase ghosts for the pleasure of it. She files them until the road gives her a second point.
