# Jaina Proudmoore / Sylvanas Windrunner

Chapter 14 is the relationship in its most painful useful form. The sanctum damage is still there. The omission is still there. And yet the road does not pause. Jaina briefs. Sylvanas listens. Neither woman gives the other what would count as repair. What they do give each other is enough truth to keep moving toward Stratholme.

The relationship now lives in spacing. Sylvanas rides half a step forward-right. Jaina stays centered. Even the brief touch of the relay stone feels functional rather than intimate. That bodily grammar matters because it replaces the easier reflex-trust of earlier chapters with something more careful and more expensive.

What keeps the bond from curdling into pure fracture is that both women still know how to work. Sylvanas pushes until the briefing becomes usable. Jaina finally answers with the ugly narrow plan. The instant that happens, Sylvanas accepts it. That acceptance is not forgiveness. It is a more limited and, in some ways, harsher form of faith: I do not trust you cleanly, but I still trust that your answer may save the city if I act on it fast enough.

In chapter 14, then, the relationship becomes deliberate persistence. The check remains attached to the trust. The trust remains attached to motion.
