# Timeline

Chapter 14 follows directly after the extraction from Caer Darrow and covers a single hard push south from late afternoon into deepening dusk. It is a road chapter in the strictest sense: no cooling stop, no real pause for recovery, and no institutional detour strong enough to break the line of movement.

The chapter's major chronological beats are clean. First comes the mounted briefing, where Jaina gives Sylvanas the larger war frame and the plague mechanism while withholding the sanctum's inner truth. Then both recall attempts land — Silvermoon through Lor'themar, Dalaran through Antonidas — and both are refused. After that, the south-track rider collapses Hearthglen's remaining slack by reporting the renewed failure and Arthas's movement on Stratholme. The chapter ends with Jaina's narrow plan and the horses driven harder into the dark.

In timeline terms, chapter 14 is the hinge between winning the answer and reaching the city where the answer may already be too late.
