# Politics

Chapter 14 is the first point where both major institutions are openly refused in the same scene. Silvermoon escalates into formal recall. Dalaran, through Antonidas, asks Jaina to come home. Neither call is answered with obedience.

That does not make the politics simple. Lor'themar remains committed to shielding Sylvanas, and Antonidas remains more mentor than bureaucrat in the moment. But the chapter locks in a new political reality: the field now holds knowledge and urgency that no institution can absorb at road speed. Procedure has become slower than the crisis in both kingdoms.

The result is not anti-political bravado. It is emergency bypass. Chapter 14 establishes that useful action now depends on the people already on the road continuing south, even when both systems above them try — for different reasons — to pull them back.
