# Did Jandice Barov get clear of Scholomance, and what damage can she still do at large?

Jandice remains loose after chapter 14, but the chapter adds an important complication: the damage she can still do is not only physical. It is interpretive. Jaina now confirms that Jandice curated her route through Scholomance, steering her past books and proofs while hiding the stock rooms and body-work Sylvanas saw. That means Jandice helped shape the partial truth now sitting inside the partnership.

This gives the loose-end thread a second edge. Yes, Jandice escaped the building. Yes, she may resurface as a witness, a survivor, or an active threat. But even before she does any of that, her hand is still present in the fallout. She is part of why Jaina's understanding of Scholomance arrived as scholarship first and atrocity second.

The thread therefore stays open on two fronts: where Jandice went, and what her curated route has already cost the people now riding south.
