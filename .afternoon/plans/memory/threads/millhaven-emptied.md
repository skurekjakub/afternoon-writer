# What happened to Millhaven?

This thread resolves in chapter 3. The answer is not pleasant but it is finally clear enough to name. Millhaven was hit by the plague in ordinary civilian channels, collapsed house by house, and then lost many of its dead to reanimation and northbound movement. The village was not emptied by evacuation, raid, or fire. It was eaten from within and then walked out of by its own dead.

What remains unresolved after that answer belongs to other threads: source, mechanism, and possible direction from outside hands. The village mystery itself is finished.
