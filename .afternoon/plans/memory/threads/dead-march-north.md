# The dead from Millhaven marched north toward Quel'Thalas

## Chapter 3 state

This is the chapter's strategic turn. The plague is no longer only a dead village behind the party. It is a moving line of danger ahead of them. Mira says the dead went north. The churn behind the houses says many bodies left on foot. The cleaner road beyond Paret's shed confirms the line held once it found the road.

The crucial unresolved piece is agency. The dead moved with enough common direction to frighten everyone in the scene, but chapter 3 still does not prove whether they are self-organizing, following some ambient pull, or being directed by living hands. That uncertainty should stay active in future chapters even while the northbound fact itself is treated as settled.
