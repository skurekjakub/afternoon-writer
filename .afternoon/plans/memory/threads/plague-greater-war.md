# What larger war is the plague preparing for?

Chapter 14 is where this thread stops belonging only to Jaina's private horror and starts belonging to the road. The answer now leaves Scholomance in spoken form. Sylvanas hears it. Antonidas hears enough of it. The larger war frame is no longer just revelation; it is active pressure on immediate decisions.

That shift matters because the chapter does not give the characters time to stand around absorbing scale. The moment the Lich King and the Burning Legion become shared field knowledge, Stratholme begins pulling the story south hard enough to keep the insight from cooling into exposition. The larger war is important precisely because it is now arriving through grain, roads, and city timetables.

The thread therefore remains open, but it is open in a new way. The cosmological chain has been named. What remains unanswered is how anyone can respond to a war this large when the next city may already be too far gone for warning alone to save it.
