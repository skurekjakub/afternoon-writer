# Fenwick Crossing

Fenwick Crossing is where the chapter stops being about one contaminated village and becomes about a region-sized road problem. The place is all shelves, wax drips, drafts, and buried autumn records, but the mood is not dusty calm. It is the pressure of information finally widening fast enough to become frightening.

This is also the depot where Sylvanas and Jaina look most like a working pair. One reads road hierarchy, the other contamination depth, and the table becomes the battlefield. Fenwick Crossing should be remembered as the first place the grain road truly unfolds.
