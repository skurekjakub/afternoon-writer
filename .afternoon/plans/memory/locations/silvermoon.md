# Silvermoon

## Chapter 1 state

Silvermoon is off-page in Chapter 1, but it is never absent. The city appears in negative space: the chamber Sylvanas does not want to enter, the clerk already setting out papers, the polished political world waiting at the north end of the road.

That matters because Silvermoon is not framed here as home in the same way Eversong is. For Sylvanas, the city is duty translated into furniture, procedure, and delay. It is where Lor'themar has to go when she rides the other way.

Continuity-wise, Chapter 1 establishes Silvermoon as the administrative center pulling against the border. Even unseen, it is the seat of the Convocation and the source of institutional pressure.
