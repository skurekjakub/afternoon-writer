# Scholomance

Chapter 14 does not return to Scholomance physically. It does something more interesting: it splits the place open in memory. Jaina remembers books, ledgers, archive space, and a curated descent designed to make the institution look like terrifying scholarship. Sylvanas remembers stock, breathing bodies, drawers of hands, sorted parts, and the hidden labor underneath the academic face. The same building now exists in two incompatible truths that are both real.

That matters because it changes the location from solved objective into active interpretive wound. The question is no longer merely what Scholomance contained. It is what each woman was allowed to see there, and what that difference means for trust afterward. Jandice's route curation becomes part of the place itself: not a social detail, but architecture weaponized into narrative control.

By chapter end, Scholomance remains behind them geographically and very much present psychologically. It is now fixed in memory as an institution with a polished academic mask and a hidden industrial body underneath, and the split between those faces is one of the reasons the road south still hurts.
