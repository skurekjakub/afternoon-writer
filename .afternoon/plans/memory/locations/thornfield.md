# Thornfield

Thornfield is still a living village node, which makes its wrongness subtler than Millhaven's. The clue here is not spectacle. It is bureaucracy gone dead in the hand: old ledgers full of useful dirt, then a cleaner hand taking over and sanding the life out of the page.

That matters because Thornfield teaches the investigation to look for administrative texture, not just plague signs. The changing scribe tells the story almost as clearly as a body would.
