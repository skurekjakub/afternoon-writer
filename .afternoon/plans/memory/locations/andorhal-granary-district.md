# Andorhal Granary District

This is the part of Andorhal where the city's false normalcy starts to show seams. Towers, loading bays, covered passages, handcarts, receipt slates, and flour-glazed stone all read as ordinary logistics until someone like Sylvanas or Havel watches the workers closely enough to notice the wrong stillness.

The district matters because both halves of the chapter's answer live here. Jaina proves the grain is being treated during packaging. Sylvanas and Havel prove the labor force includes a hidden cult cell with leaders, a rear lane, and an off-ledger building.

Write it as industrial routine stretched over conspiracy. Nothing about it should feel theatrically evil at first glance.
