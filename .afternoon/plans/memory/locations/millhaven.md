# Millhaven

Millhaven remains the road's first completed catastrophe. Chapter 3 made it a full wound on the map: intact houses, missing bodies, contamination in every domestic layer, and one survivor hidden below the taint. Chapter 4 changes its continuity function rather than its physical state. Millhaven becomes the name that can force a living village to stop lying to itself.

That matters because the place is no longer only evidence for Jaina and Sylvanas. It has become a benchmark. When Sylvanas says We came from Millhaven, she is effectively carrying the future of the current village into the square and making everyone look at it. In continuity terms, Millhaven is now both a dead location and a live warning.
