# Southern Border Outpost

The southern border outpost is where Quel'Thalas turns suspicion into process. In chapter 1 it is a secured post. In chapter 2 it becomes something more revealing: proof that Farstrider custody and Farstrider deployment are separated by only a few orders.

The place is built out of white border stone, ward-light, disciplined yards, and practical military habits. The holding room is close enough to the active defenses that the hum gets into the prisoner's body. Outside, there is a tack shelter, spare weapons, horses already being brought up, and no wasted motion once Sylvanas decides the threat is real.

Use this location as a threshold space. It is not homey, and it is not theatrical. It is the machinery of sovereignty: wards, protocol, equipment, and the speed with which authority can become action.
