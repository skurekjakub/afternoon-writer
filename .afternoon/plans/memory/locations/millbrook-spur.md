# Millbrook Spur

Millbrook Spur is the chapter's cleaned wound. It should have been loud with freight and clerks. Instead it offers blank books, a staged front office, an empty yard, and a dead overseer hidden just far enough back to be missed by a shallow search.

This is the depot where the enemy's counterwork becomes undeniable. The recovered slate and route scrap matter, but the deeper continuity function is that Millbrook Spur proves the road is being curated against pursuit. It is not only carrying plague. It is managing witnesses.
