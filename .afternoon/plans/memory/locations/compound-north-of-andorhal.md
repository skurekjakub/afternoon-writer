# Compound north of Andorhal

## Core read

This compound is the chapter's hardest location because it is built to flatter expertise. The dip on the north side looks exactly like the sort of seam a ranger-general should trust. That is the trap. The enemy has started writing magical intent into terrain that already looks tactically persuasive.

## What happened here

Lyriel dies here, instantly, without the kind of heroic spacing fiction often grants to supporting scouts. That matters. The location should be remembered as the point where fieldcraft alone proved insufficient and Jaina's sweep became command-critical rather than merely useful.

## Carry-forward use

Writers returning to this place or to later Eastweald sites should preserve its design logic: low rise, clean yard, attractive seam, hidden wards, living support teams, and a price extracted from any formation that moves before the magic is fully read.
