# Three Willows

## Chapter 3 state

Three Willows exists in Chapter 3 as named future danger. Paret places it farther north along the same road, after Alder Run. That alone is enough to make it continuity-relevant: it is one of the settlements implied to still be alive while the dead are already moving.

Until the prose reaches it, treat Three Willows as part of the warning chain rather than as a fully described setting. Its main function is geographic and dramatic. It widens the road ahead.
