# Silvermoon City

## Overview
Silvermoon does not get a full scene in chapter 1, but it still exerts pressure. It is the city waiting north of the road: chamber, precedent, and everything Sylvanas distrusts about letting urgency pass through too many hands.

## Chapter-1 meaning
In this chapter, Silvermoon is less a visual setting than a counterforce. The border is living work. Silvermoon is where that work gets translated into argument. Lor'themar goes there because someone has to. Sylvanas avoids it because she knows exactly what it costs her.
