The Grounding Details
I broke the grounding pass down into four main categories: Geography, Factions & Politics, Magic & Mechanics, and Sensory/Physical Textures.

1. Geography & Locations
Instead of just saying "the road south" or "the country," I anchored the movement to the map of Lordaeron:

The King's Road

The Scholomance basin and Lordamere Lake

The Eastweald and Tirisfal Glades

Darrowshire crossroads

Silverpine and Alterac mountains

Stratholme specific districts: The central market, the baker's district, warehouse lanes.

2. Factions & Politics
Vague terms like "the rest," "the hand above them," or "they" were replaced with the actual power players and hierarchies:

The Cult of the Damned and The Burning Legion

King Terenas's kingdom * The Kirin Tor, The Violet Citadel, and The Council of Six

The Convocation of Silvermoon, the Sunstrider throne, Grand Magister Rommath, and Lord Saltheril

The Silver Hand cavalry and the First Legion infantry led by Uther and Arthas.

3. Magic Systems & Mechanics
"The sickness" or "the process" is a hallmark of lazy AI writing. I defined how the magic actually worked on a biological and arcane level:

Necrotic carrier / payload and shadow magic

Runic formulas and arcane anchors (phylactery ritual)

Hive-mind directive controlled by Ner'zhul (the Lich King)

Evocation frost/fire

Explaining that elven Runestones of Quel'Thalas only detect external magic, not digested necrotic compounds.

4. Sensory & Tangible Textures
Replacing generic items with universe-specific gear and materials:

Alchemical chalk instead of just "chalk"

Lordamere lake water and leather canteen

Thalassian leatherwork / elven armor

A Kirin Tor sending stone with mithril filigree and violet light

Mahogany desk and a cup of tea instead of a "side table" and "cold cup."

Iron greaves and blue Lordaeron cloaks for the courier.
