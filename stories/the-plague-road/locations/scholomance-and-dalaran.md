# Scholomance and Dalaran — Location Notes

- Use this for the academic mirror: one school that refused the line and one that drew it too late.

## Caer Darrow
- Island fortress in Darrowmere, restored by the Barovs and handed to Kel'Thuzad for the price of promised immortality.
- Surface should read abandoned, cold, and wrong rather than theatrically ruined.
- The lake approach matters: mist, still water, and necromantic saturation Jaina can feel before she lands.

## Scholomance
- Built beneath the keep: crypts, lecture rooms, labs, archive space, Kel'Thuzad's office.
- The horror is not chaos but order. It should feel like a real academy with the ethics removed.
- Key sensory line: old stone, preservatives, torch smoke, sweet-rot, chanting too far away to map cleanly.
- Kel'Thuzad's study matters because it is neat. That neatness is the blade.

## Dalaran
- Arcane city, clean lines, high towers, ambient magical hum, and a culture built around knowledge and deliberation.
- It is one of the few human places where an elf feels naturally at ease.
- The beauty of it matters because Jaina is carrying that beauty into places built from the same methods for worse ends.

## Violet Citadel
- Antonidas's ground: council rooms, library, labs, private quarters, dangerous vaults.
- Important less as geography and more as the sound and pressure behind the stone calls.
- Through the sending stone, Jaina should hear Dalaran as wards, argument, books, and controlled power.

## Contrast rule
- Dalaran: brilliant, slow, institutional, polished.
- Scholomance: brilliant, fast, forbidden, polished in the same places and foul in all the others.
