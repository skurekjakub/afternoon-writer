# The Lure — How Arthas Reaches Frostmourne

- The tragedy is that truth helps damn him.
- S&J give Arthas real coordinates. He turns them into a road north.

## Chain
- Ch 15: he learns the plague has a designer, a northern source, and a target that feels reachable.
- Stratholme removes every brake: Uther gone, Jaina gone, institutions useless.
- Mal'Ganis curates real information at the right moments and stays invisible.
- Ner'zhul amplifies what is already in Arthas instead of replacing it. The voice is felt as rightness, not orders.
- The cave only closes a choice made earlier.

## Important constraints
- Arthas is destroyed by true facts with missing meanings, not by fake facts.
- S&J do not know about Frostmourne before the cave.
- Mal'Ganis makes sure Arthas gets the trail before they do.
- Ship-burning is the final expression of a pattern already visible at Stratholme.

## Sea note
- The Lich King pushes sustained headwinds at Jaina's ship because she is the threat.
- That cost leaves her more sensitive to the blade later.
