# Frostmourne — The Seeking

- Arthas is dead. The blade is not between owners; it is hunting for the next one.
- It does this by reading nearby hunger, grief, duty, ambition, and emptiness, then answering with certainty.

## Progression
- Hours 1-6: wrong cold, pressure, instinct to leave.
- Hours 6-24: whispers begin.
- Days 1-3: tailored dreams start.
- Days 3-14: cold and influence spread beyond the vault. More people start circling back.
- Week 3+: one candidate is likely to reach for the chest if nothing changes.

## Likely hands
- Ser Edric Valecrest: ambitious young knight who wants to matter.
- Grieving steward: wants his dead son back.
- Devout initiate: mistakes the blade's cadence for something that sounds like the Light.
- None of them are villains. That is the point.

## Why containment fails
- Palace wards were built for cursed objects, not an active channel.
- The blade learns the wards by resonance and starts slipping through them.
- Palace mages are already behind and Kirin Tor access is blocked.

## Who knows
- Lor'themar's elves can feel the vault as violation, but they arrive without authority.
- Jaina can hear the seeking clearly from her cell and knows it is working.
- No one believes the prisoner who understands the sword best.

## Book 2 clock
- Give it three to four weeks with no intervention and someone opens the chest.
- The claiming should be quiet, not theatrical: night shift, cracked seals, one hand on the latch, one heartbeat of false warmth.
