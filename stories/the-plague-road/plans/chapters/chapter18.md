# Chapter 18: The Wall

**POV:** Jaina Proudmoore (limited third, absolute)
**When:** Directly follows Chapter 17. Opens on Stratholme's inner wall after the Culling and closes on the south road with the north question newly alive.
**Where:** Stratholme's inner defensive wall above the main gate.
**Travel:** Static witness position on the wall -> descent on foot -> horseback south out of the city.
**Characters:** Jaina, Sylvanas, Cyndia.
**Goal:** Survive the wall, absorb what Stratholme taught her, and move before the knowledge turns into paralysis she cannot leave.

## What we know

**What Jaina knows at open**
- The source plan was real and worked locally, but Stratholme had already pushed too much contaminated grain into ovens, homes, and bodies.
- Arthas answered that failure with the Culling and carried it through to the end.
- Sylvanas witnessed the failed source attempt before the wall, which means the Scholomance scar is under direct pressure from field evidence instead of argument.
- The plague's activation leaves a city-scale residue different from anything Jaina has yet observed.
- Arthas came out of the gate with clean hands.

**What Jaina doesn't know yet**
- Whether the source model now points to a single broadcaster, a relay chain, or something else entirely.
- Where Arthas goes next.
- Whether Antonidas can still speak to the person she is after Stratholme.
- Whether "home" still means anything usable once she leaves this wall.

**What NOT to do:**
- No explicit Frostmourne identification.
- No Sylvanas interiority.
- No full Dalaran severance yet; that belongs to Chapter 19.
- No softening of the Culling into something noble because it prevented a wider turn.

**What they learn by the end**
- The shared wall witness welds the crack between Jaina and Sylvanas without either woman naming it.
- Antonidas reaches Jaina after Stratholme, but the call ends in distance, not resolution.
- Jaina's countermeasure model now points past contaminated grain toward a source or relay that broadcasts the activating frequency.
- A Farstrider relay confirms Arthas is sailing north for Northrend with a loyalty-selected force.
- The chapter ends with the question alive but unspoken: do they keep riding south, or do they turn north after him?

## Character Arcs

### Jaina
- If she can understand Stratholme precisely enough, she can still salvage something from it besides ash.
- Stay upright long enough to turn horror into usable knowledge and then move when motion becomes possible again.
- Arthas's clean hands, the city-scale residue, the certainty question opened by Kel'Thuzad and Arthas both, and Sylvanas's silent presence beside her.
- Jaina still reaches for explanation as if understanding might reduce the cost already paid.
- The chapter asks whether she can let witness, grief, and analysis occupy the same space without letting any one of them erase the others.
- Stay on the wall and keep processing, or let Sylvanas pull her back into movement before the wall becomes the only thing she can do.
- Jaina moves from "the countermeasure failed at city scale" to "the countermeasure was aimed at the wrong layer; the true answer lies farther north."
- Chapter 19 opens with the source model pointing north and both institutions moving to pull these women in the opposite direction.

### Arthas through Jaina's POV
- Former lover, prince, and terminal example of conviction without friction.
- **POV pressure:** Jaina reads him from inside the old history rather than from military distance.
- He is physically gone after the opening image. The chapter works on his absence, the wall, and what his last visible choices mean.

### Partnership / team
- Damaged pair on the edge of becoming something harder to break.
- Shared witness finishes the repair that the source attempt started. The proof is not verbal. It lives in touch, timing, and the missing verification beat afterward.
- No speeches about trust. No narrator summary that outpaces what Jaina can actually observe.

## Scene 1: The Wall

**What happens:** Hold Jaina on the wall long enough for the Culling to become personal, magical, and irreversible rather than merely historical.
**Characters:** Jaina, Sylvanas, distant purge teams below
**Where we start:** The city is dead, the source plan failed by timing rather than bad theory, and Arthas has already come through the gate with clean hands.

### Beat 1
- The silence after the Culling reads differently from ordinary emptiness because a city this large should still be making human noise
- Jaina's body recognizes the city-scale residue before her mind has turned it into theory. Sylvanas remains beside her in working silence rather than trying to force speech into the scene. They do not know whether she can move under her own power once the wall stops being the whole world.
- What, exactly, the residue will teach her; Warm parapet stone under both palms; Fire eating through buildings block by block instead of one general blaze; a silence made louder by the ordinary city hum no longer being there.
### Beat 2
- Arthas's concern with presentation survived intact all the way to atrocity
- The Alterac ruling now reads as a small-scale version of the same fatal gap between speed and judgment. Jaina's old diagnosis was accurate and useless at once. They do not know whether being right about Arthas can ever do more than mark the shape of the damage or how much of this reading comes from history and how much from the wall.
- Pale hands catching firelight with no blood on them; the memory of a circlet straightened in a mirror; Heat on Jaina's face from a city her science could not save.
### Beat 3
- Stratholme gives Jaina the first true city-scale activation read she has ever had
- The contamination's behavior at scale differs from village and laboratory models. The countermeasure was not false; it was built for smaller, cleaner problems than this city. They do not know what the broader source model actually is or whether the new information will matter soon enough to save anyone else.
- Bread-taste wrongness in the back of her throat; the residue fading like a fire that already spent its fuel; Smoke, ash, and necromantic drag layered into one air.

## Scene 2: The Diagnosis

**What happens:** Push Jaina from "Arthas failed" to the uglier question of what conviction looks like when she shares its structure.
**Characters:** Jaina, Sylvanas beside her, Arthas and Kel'Thuzad as mental reference points only
**Where we start:** Stratholme proved the city-scale failure of the existing plan and gave Jaina new data anyway.

### Beat 4
- Jaina saw the pattern years earlier in a context small enough to dismiss
- Leaving Arthas solved the relationship but changed nothing about his underlying trajectory. The wall turns a remembered argument into a mass grave's receipt. They do not know whether staying would actually have changed him or whether that question matters anymore.
- White knuckles on the parapet; Heat coming through stone from the fires below; the remembered stillness of a prince who already thought the decision finished.
### Beat 5
- Kel'Thuzad removed ethical friction in the name of understanding
- Arthas removed deliberative friction in the name of duty. Jaina sees a version of the same structure in her own habit of trusting correct analysis to justify continued forward motion. They do not know what separates her from them in practice rather than intent or whether Stratholme just gave her the answer in the form of the woman standing beside her.
- Bread-taste lingering beneath smoke; Kel'Thuzad's chalk remembered in the hand that no longer holds it; Sylvanas's unbroken stillness at Jaina's left shoulder.

## Scene 3: The Anchor

**What happens:** Break the paralysis physically and let Jaina observe the partnership change through movement rather than confession.
**Characters:** Jaina, Sylvanas, Cyndia
**Where we start:** The wall has already given Jaina more thought than she can use here.

### Beat 6
- Sylvanas waits until touch can work before using it
- The hand on Jaina's shoulder functions as direction, not consolation. "South. We go south." is both route instruction and temporary permission to stop staring. They do not know whether south is only escape or the actual next path or how much of the wall Jaina is carrying with her now that she can move.
- Leather gauntlet on Jaina's left shoulder; Heat still trapped in the parapet stone as she turns away from it; Smoke caught in both their clothes.
### Beat 7
- Cyndia has horses, water, and tack ready without asking for language from either of them
- Jaina rides again in the same left-rear position from the head-start run into Stratholme. Sylvanas no longer inserts the fractional pause that followed Scholomance before acting on Jaina's lane. They do not know what they will do with the welded partnership once the road stops being pure aftermath or whether Jaina can name the change before the next crisis uses it.
- Worn watch-steps under boots; Sylvanas's back below her on the stair, giving the eye somewhere to fix; Horse tack already checked and waiting at the base.

## Scene 4: The Road and the Pivot

**What happens:** Carry the trio out of Stratholme, keep the Antonidas line unresolved but alive, evolve the source model, and let north enter the story as the next real direction.
**Characters:** Jaina, Sylvanas, Cyndia, Antonidas via sending-stone, Farstrider relay rider
**Where we start:** The crack is practically welded, but nothing else about Stratholme is resolved.

### Beat 8
- Antonidas already has fragmentary word of Stratholme
- He can offer extraction immediately if she asks for it. Jaina can only give him "alive" and "not yet," because the north answer has not finished forming. They do not know whether Jaina will eventually go back through that line or close it herself or whether Antonidas will still sound like home once the next choice is made.
- Sending-stone warmth in her palm after the call ends; Smoke column still visible behind the riders while she speaks; Saddlebag weight once the stone goes back in beside notes and samples.
### Beat 9
- Grain is not the true instrument of the plague; it amplifies a frequency coming from somewhere else
- City-scale activation behaves like a network responding to a broadcast, not a simple chain reaction. Kel'Thuzad's death removed one relay, not the whole system. They do not know what object, place, or being is broadcasting locally now or whether the northbound force is hunting the source, serving it, or both.
- Frequency carried in the body like a melody she cannot stop hearing; Bread-taste fading with distance while the remembered pattern stays sharp; Notes in her lap while the horse keeps moving under them.
### Beat 10
- Arthas is commandeering ships for Northrend
- Uther rides west to warn Terenas. The force sailing north consists of men who stayed through Stratholme and proved they will follow him past moral collapse. They do not know whether pursuit is possible with the resources they still have or whether north means source, trap, or both.
- Relay horse lathered at the chest; Dispatch tube slapped into Jaina's or Sylvanas's waiting hand; Dusk road wind starting to cool with open water somewhere ahead.
### Beat 11
- The chapter ends with south as immediate motion and north as actual pressure
- Jaina's revised model and the relay report now point in the same direction. The next chapter can turn from aftermath into choice. They do not know who will say "north" first or what each woman is willing to lose to mean it.
- Evening road under hooves; Stratholme's smoke flattening into a stain on the horizon behind them; the sending-stone still faintly warm in the saddlebag while the north answer forms around it.
