# Chapter 13: The Arrival

**POV:** Sylvanas Windrunner (limited third, absolute)
**When:** Directly follows Chapter 12. Opens with Sylvanas and Cyndia reaching Caer Darrow while Jaina is still in Kel'Thuzad's sanctum, and ends on the road south after the clean exit and the first Hearthglen relay.
**Where:** Darrowmere shoreline opposite Caer Darrow.
**Travel:** Mounted approach before open; rowboat across Darrowmere; on foot for the breach, descent, sanctum fight, and exit.
**Characters:** Sylvanas, Cyndia.
**Goal:** Get into Scholomance, find Jaina, and get her back out before whatever runs the place closes around her.

## What we know

**What Sylvanas knows at open**
- Jaina entered the Barov estate and never came back out
- Cyndia is the only remaining wing with her, and Lyriel's absence now shapes every split decision
- The estate is occupied, heavily warded, and wrong in the way a university turned necromantic worksite would be wrong
- Jaina took the Barov trail while Sylvanas took the Arthas march, so Sylvanas is arriving late and blind.
- Whatever waits below is arcane enough that Sylvanas can feel its pressure without being able to name its mechanics

**What Sylvanas doesn't know yet**
- What Jaina found in Kel'Thuzad's sanctum
- Whether Jaina is captive, compromised, or still below on purpose
- The full resonance model, the animation trigger, the Lich King chain, or the Burning Legion frame
- Whether Kel'Thuzad expects to run, fight, or simply keep talking if she reaches him
- How she will read the room once she gets there

**What NOT to do**
- No correct reading of Jaina's trial in Chapter 12
- No betrayal language or open accusation
- No repair of the split partnership
- No tribunal or institutional aftermath
- No explicit Mal'Ganis identification

**What they learn by the end**
- Kel'Thuzad is dead, Jandice is loose, and the Barov line is finished as a living Scholomance shield
- Jaina is carrying the full plague spell, the countermeasure, and the Lich King / Burning Legion chain out of Scholomance
- Sylvanas leaves with a hard misread of what happened in the sanctum, and Jaina lets her keep it.
- Cyndia rejoins them; the formation is three women now, with Lyriel's absence on the right and a new half-step crack in the middle
- Hearthglen is already overwhelmed and Arthas is moving toward Stratholme, so the road south starts immediately

## Scene 1: The Approach

**What happens:** Sylvanas and Cyndia breach Caer Darrow.
**Characters:** Sylvanas, Cyndia
**Where we start:** Jaina went in and did not come out; the estate is occupied and the real source lies across the lake.

### Beat 1
- The deepest source is on Caer Darrow, not in the manor surface alone
- Jaina reached the shore, took a boat, and left no return line behind her. The lake crossing itself is part of the estate's security, not just the road to the island. They do not know what is holding the island together from below or whether Jaina is trapped, negotiating, or already dead.
- Mist sitting low over green-black water; an outer ward shimmer hanging over the lake like a skin; Rowboats pulled half out of the mud beside an abandoned landing.
### Beat 2
- The east-face window gives Sylvanas an entry route.
- Cyndia holds the boat, the breach point, and the relay line. Lyriel's absence is now built into the shape of every decision. They do not know what waits on the island's upper levels or whether Sylvanas will find Jaina quickly enough.
- Oarlocks wrapped in cloth so the crossing stays quiet; Mist closing behind the boat as soon as they push off; a brief softening in the ward pressure near the east wall below the window; Cyndia's automatic right-side check before she settles in place; Wet stone under Sylvanas's hands as she takes the window climb.

## Scene 2: The Descent

**What happens:** Sylvanas breaches the window, avoids the public path, ambushes Jandice, and descends to the sanctum.
**Characters:** Sylvanas, Jandice
**Where we start:** Sylvanas is on the island alone.

### Beat 3
- Scholomance's upper levels still carry the bones of the Barov estate.
- Side rooms hold peasant bodies in different stages of conversion. Reanimated hands and other stripped parts are being worked as separate test stock. Jaina's trail keeps going down. They do not know who designed the deeper defenses or what condition Jaina is in.
- Lake water dripping from the sill; Blue-white fungi pulsing brighter; a peasant hand scratching uselessly at a table edge; Dusty archive air turning to preservative cold; Chemical preservation over an old-rot smell; Cold that feels like heat is being pulled out of the stone.
### Beat 4
- Jandice heard enough to know Jaina was asking real questions
- Kel'Thuzad valued the exchange enough to dismiss his own student. Jandice makes plain that Jaina was given the polished route. The older crypt stair below the upper research levels is the fast way down. They do not know how much of Jandice's framing is jealousy or what the sanctum itself will show once Sylvanas reaches it.
- Jandice's reagent-burned hands under Sylvanas's grip; Fungi-light catching chalk dust in the seams of a scholar's sleeve; Anger in Jandice's voice that sounds insulted before it sounds afraid.
### Beat 5
- The lower route passes discard rooms and holding spaces
- Jaina and Kel'Thuzad are speaking in an academic cadence. The sanctum door is standing open. Kel'Thuzad still sounds calm. They do not know what the room itself will show once Sylvanas enters or whether Jaina's face will match the voice Sylvanas is hearing.
- Bone-dry dust on the older stair under newer ward-ripple light; a jaw still moving in a bucket of preservative; Kel'Thuzad's lecture cadence carrying through the stone; Jaina's lower, more analytical register answering it; Corridor cold tightening Sylvanas's bow hand.

## Scene 3: The Room

**What happens:** Sylvanas sees a tableau that anyone would misread.
**Characters:** Sylvanas, Jaina, Kel'Thuzad
**Where we start:** Jandice's complaint and the voices say the conversation was real.

### Beat 6
- Jaina is positioned in engagement posture
- Jaina has chalk dust on her fingers. They do not know whether the notation in Jaina's hand is counterargument, collaboration, or both or what happened in the hours before Sylvanas reached the room.
- Chalk dust on Jaina's left hand; Cold tea on a side table near Jaina; Fungi-light flattening the room.
### Beat 7
- Kel'Thuzad valued the exchange enough that Jandice envied it
- After the holding rooms above, the sanctum's clean office reads as refinement. The critical missing fact lives inside Jaina. They do not know whether Jaina will be able to challenge this read without making it worse.
- That Jaina fought the argument for hours; That the chalk marks in Jaina's hand were part of a counterargument; the cold cup near Jaina's place at the table; Two hands visible on the boards; Sylvanas's body going still in the particular way it does before a new file is sealed.
### Beat 8
- Jaina sees the shift happen
- They do not know whether any later explanation can narrow the gap or how much this new watchfulness will alter their ride.
- Kel'Thuzad's calm voice; Sylvanas's total stillness; Silence settling over the sanctum.

## Scene 4: The Partnership Activates

**What happens:** The pack still works in combat, and Kel'Thuzad dies as the scholar he has been all chapter.
**Characters:** Sylvanas, Jaina, Kel'Thuzad
**Where we start:** Sylvanas has filed the misread and Jaina knows it.

### Beat 9
- Sylvanas takes the northeast lane and uses it for the throat shot
- Both women verify before they commit. They do not know whether the fight's clean finish can keep this new watchfulness from following them or what Kel'Thuzad will do with his final breath.
- Ice-lattice barriers collapsing the exits; Bowstring note lowered by the cold; the fraction of extra space Jaina leaves in the barrier pattern.
### Beat 10
- He expected death and priced it into the continuity of his work
- Sylvanas notices the calm and files it as evidence. They do not know how useful Kel'Thuzad's last line will be or whether Jaina can carry both the intelligence and the room's damage out.
- The arrow in Kel'Thuzad's throat; Kel'Thuzad's hands staying on the table; Fungi-light pulsing once.
---

## Scene 5: The Ascent

**What happens:** them out of Scholomance, re-form the three-woman line, and show the crack as spacing, timing, and silence rather than argument.
**Characters:** Sylvanas, Jaina, Cyndia
**Where we start:** Kel'Thuzad is dead, Jaina has the intelligence, and neither woman has said the thing that matters.

### Beat 11
- Sylvanas now ranges closer to Jaina than she did before the sanctum
- Jaina reads the escort spacing and does not challenge it. Jandice is already gone from the upper levels by the time they climb out. They do not know whether Sylvanas will ever share how much Jandice's complaint shaped the room-read or whether Jaina will try to explain the sanctum before they reach the next crisis.
- Fungi-light dimmer on the way up than it was on the way down; Pressure in the walls shifting as the building redistributes around Kel'Thuzad's absence; Jaina's silence carrying more weight than her footfalls.
### Beat 12
- Cyndia's relay says Hearthglen is overwhelmed and Arthas is already moving toward Stratholme
- The re-formed line is three women now, with Lyriel's absence on the right and Sylvanas's half-step oversight in the middle. Jaina will give the intelligence product in Chapter 14 without giving the sanctum's internal truth. They do not know whether the crack can be repaired before the next catastrophe forces them deeper together again or whether any institution will hear the intelligence in time to matter.
- Late light on contaminated Eastweald soil; Cyndia's second right-side check when she falls back into line; Sylvanas's half-step forward changing the whole feel of the road.
