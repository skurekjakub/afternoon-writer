# The Plague Road — Story Arc

- Genre: competence thriller that hardens into tragedy.
- Tone: Ch 1-6 witty procedural; Ch 7-14 darker conspiracy; Ch 15-18 atrocity and rupture; Ch 19-25 lean endgame thriller.
- POV: Sylvanas odd chapters, Jaina even chapters, with dual exceptions in Ch 22 and Ch 25.
- End state: they kill Arthas in the throne room, save no one who can testify, and are condemned anyway.

## Premise
- Sylvanas catches Jaina inside the ward boundary, treats her as a threat, then discovers the mage is the only person reading the plague correctly.
- Their hostile investigation becomes a chase from border villages to Andorhal, Scholomance, Stratholme, Northrend, and back to Capital City.
- They kill Arthas seconds after he murders Terenas and are found standing over both bodies.

## Core engine
- Two authorities forced together: military tactician vs arcane prodigy.
- Main relationship line: threat -> asset -> partner -> necessary -> absolute trust under fire.
- Every solved problem reveals a bigger, faster, less containable one.

## The four spines
### 1. External plot
- Trace how the plague is getting in.
- Break Cult infrastructure.
- Track Arthas through Stratholme and Northrend.
- Kill him before he can fully consolidate the throne.

### 2. Partnership
- See partnership-arc.md.
- The emotional core is trust earned through repeated correct action.

### 3. Sylvanas's line
- Lie: the world beyond the border is someone else's problem.
- Truth: the border is political fiction once the threat ignores it.
- Hardest turn: she defies the recall and chooses the mission anyway.

### 4. Jaina's line
- Lie: being right and brilliant is enough.
- Truth: intelligence without trust turns monstrous fast.
- Her mirror pair is Kel'Thuzad in method and Arthas in conviction. The partnership is what keeps her from their shape.

## Major mirrors
- Kel'Thuzad = Jaina's methodological dark mirror.
- Arthas = conviction without counterweight.
- Antonidas and Lor'themar = good men losing to systems built to delay.
- Mal'Ganis = invisible counterintelligence turning correct investigation into late investigation.

## World rules
- High elves cannot be reanimated by the plague in this AU.
- That fact makes Silvermoon complacent, not safe.
- Mal'Ganis sanitizes sites, steers Arthas, and hides behind a repeated road-farewell.
- Frostmourne is not a solved problem when Arthas dies.

## Arc map
- Investigation, Ch 1-8: border breach to Andorhal.
- Scholomance, Ch 9-14: split, dark mirror, crack.
- Arthas, Ch 15-18: Hearthglen to Stratholme.
- Endgame, Ch 19-25: Northrend, return, throne room, condemnation.
