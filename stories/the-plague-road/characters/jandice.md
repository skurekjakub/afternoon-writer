# Jandice Barov

- Role: Jaina's guide into Scholomance, peer mirror, the choice made now instead of decades later.
- Core: Kel'Thuzad is the lifetime mirror; Jandice is the hallway mirror.

## Look
- Same-age peer echo for Jaina: scholar's posture, reagent-burned hands, academic training visible in the body.
- Barov colors over Kirin Tor method. The split between noble daughter and true believer should show on sight.

## Voice
- Curated noble-guide voice at first.
- Then the scholarly break: longer, faster, brighter, too interested to keep pretending the work is ordinary.
- What she wants from Jaina is not approval but recognition.

## What matters
- Alexei Barov made a bargain. Jandice made a choice.
- She genuinely believes the work is rigorous enough to justify itself.
- Her blind spot is not stupidity. It is selective attention: method in one room, consequence in another.

## Ch 10 use
- The tour is a chess game.
- Her job is to contain what Jaina sees.
- Her pride keeps making her show too much.
- The handwriting and notation mirror should land hard: same training family, different moral exit.

## Book 2 pressure
- Once the dead march in full view, her wall between elegant work and monstrous cost cannot hold forever.
- Useful futures: turncoat, rationalizing heir, or shattered witness. Pick one and commit.
