## Silvermoon and early-investigation support
### Magistrix Eressea
- Convocation liaison.
- Formal, ornate, politically frictionless in a way Sylvanas hates on sight.
- Use her when the institution needs a polite face.

### Apprentice Maric
- Nervous Kirin Tor courier.
- Young, over-formal, clearly out of his depth.
- Good for showing how badly the official response lags the actual threat.

## Lordaeron and throne-room support
### King Terenas
- Elderly, kind, long-practiced kingly authority.
- In the throne room he gets only what matters: relief, confusion, death, crown on marble.

### Captain Falric
- Arthas's loyal lieutenant.
- Personal loyalty over judgment.
- Use him as the human face of following the prince too far.

### Lieutenant Marwyn
- Falric's quieter counterpart.
- Present for texture, chain of command, and the widening army cost.

### Captain Gavren Marsh
- Palace guard officer who arrests S&J.
- Honest, competent, and devastating because he simply reports what the room looked like.

### Lord Magistrate Aldous Thornton
- Presiding judge.
- Fair by ordinary standards, utterly unfit for truth at this scale.

### Lord Prosecutor Reynard Ashcombe
- Smart enough to build the wrong case beautifully.
- Does not lie. He arranges true facts into a cleaner false story.

## Barov / Scholomance support
### Lord Alexei Barov
- Made the bargain for estate, safety, and survival.
- Courteous, old-money, transactional rather than ideologically committed.

### The Barov family
- Minor nobility whose land and grain network let the plague scale.
- Useful as the face of ordinary elite complicity.

## Investigation support
### Ranger Lyriel
- Cyndia's partner.
- Quietly reliable until the trap kills her.
- Her death matters because the reader has stopped noticing her.

### Ranger Neral
- Early third ranger and northbound dispatch rider.
- Structural use: gets warnings out and escorts the survivor.

### Apprentice Corin
- Junior Kirin Tor contact in Andorhal.
- Nervous, over-prepared, actually excellent at the job.
- Proves the institution can still produce useful people even while the hierarchy fails.

### Captain Aldric Havel
- Andorhal militia captain.
- Harried, honest, solving the wrong scale of problem because no one told him the right one.

### Captured operative
- Ch 8 prisoner who dies before he can finish talking.
- Use as the hard turn from hunt to hunted.

## Travel and Northrend support
### Captain Dagny Ironhull
- Ship captain for the Northrend run.
- Blunt, practical, civilian witness to what S&J now carry.

### Baelgun Flamebeard
- Dwarven commander who can take Muradin once the cave goes bad.
- Short, solid, functional presence.

## Book 2 seed
### Ser Edric Valecrest
- Young palace guard officer and most likely next hand for Frostmourne.
- Not a villain. Ambitious, decent, too hungry to be invisible.
- Important because the blade's next victim should have a face, a name, and a mother who polished his buckle.
