# Muradin Bronzebeard

- Role: Northrend ally, second mentor Arthas betrays, Frostmourne's first casualty.
- Core: blunt, decent, curious for the cleanest reasons in the book, which is why his fate lands so hard.

## Look
- Broad, weathered, iron-ringed beard, old hammer, expedition armor kept functional rather than fine.
- He should read as a man who has been hit by everything and found most of it survivable.

## Voice
- Short declarative sentences, thick Ironforge flavor, profanity used like punctuation.
- Gruff warmth instead of softness.
- He explains only what survival requires.

## What matters
- Trained Arthas to fight and liked the boy before the prince became a machine.
- Reads S&J's partnership instantly and adjusts what he shares because he trusts real field pairs.
- Came north for discovery, not power, which makes Frostmourne's price on him feel especially cruel.

## Arthas line
- Trainer: saw the speed problem early.
- Ally: watches the prince grow narrower and more dangerous in Northrend.
- Victim: warns him off the blade, gets struck down anyway, and is left behind without a backward glance.

## Chapter use
- Ch 21: local intel, Frostmourne warning, first outside voice naming how tight S&J are as a team.
- Ch 22: wounded and left with Baelgun while the real chase runs on.

## Writer rule
- Keep him plainspoken. His usefulness comes from clarity, not from speeches.
