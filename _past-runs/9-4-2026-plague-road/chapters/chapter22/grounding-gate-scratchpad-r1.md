# Grounding Gate Scratchpad — chapter22 iteration 1

Verdict: FAIL

## KEEP decisions

### KEEP 1 — Baelgun's yard still reads as load-bearing dwarven triage
- **Category:** place / handoff infrastructure
- **Excerpt:** "No carved gate. No challenge trench. Just a yard stamped hard by dwarf boots and stretcher drags..."
- **Defense applied:** World-specific expedition-country materiality
- **Why it survived:** The camp remains a working casualty yard instead of generic snow-camp scenery. Forge trench, hide roofs, stretcher drag, iron-clasped beard, and the immediate treatment hustle all keep the threshold materially dwarven.
- **G5 check:** The passage does not depend on "Muradin's camp" as a label; labor pattern, weather exposure, and treatment gear make the place actionable on page.

### KEEP 2 — Jaina's seam work is still mostly carried in timed body cues
- **Category:** embodied magic
- **Excerpt:** "Take. / Throw. / A hitch she could count through clenched teeth. She wrote it in the air. Three short marks for the inward pull. The hook for the turn. The pause where the rhythm changed hands."
- **Defense applied:** Motion before explanation
- **Why it survived:** The strongest seam beats still live in teeth, fingers, timing, and repetition rather than abstract theorem language. Even where some hinge lines drift upward, the core mechanism work remains bodily enough to defend.

### KEEP 3 — Daggercap Bay keeps its callback identity through matter, not nostalgia
- **Category:** place callback fidelity
- **Excerpt:** "Daylight laid the harbor bare again: black wreckage frozen into the edge, hacked docks, ropes gone stiff as iron..."
- **Defense applied:** Callback place grounded through fresh physical evidence
- **Why it survived:** The harbor returns as damaged infrastructure with labor underway, not as a name-drop. Burn scars, cracked ice, handspikes, curses, pitch, and char all keep the bay continuous with prior chapters.
- **G5 check:** "Daggercap" is not doing the work alone; the harbor reads clearly through wreckage, dock labor, and smell-profile.

### KEEP 4 — The crossing's labor stays concrete and purchased
- **Category:** travel / ship materiality
- **Excerpt:** "Jaina stood at the stern when ordered, one hand braced against the rail slick with spray and old salt... Then food shoved into her hands... Then down again when blood started dripping over the deck planks."
- **Defense applied:** Cost carried through deck labor
- **Why it survived:** The southbound speed is paid for in rail, spray, fish, nosebleeds, rag work, and crew discipline. The chapter does not let the voyage become abstract urgency for long; it usually remembers the ship as machinery and Jaina as a spent component inside it.

### KEEP 5 — Lordaeron's false calm remains civic and inhabited
- **Category:** public-normalcy grounding
- **Excerpt:** "Goldenrod garlands looped from fence post to fence post. Someone had tied blue ribbons around the square pump. A painted board outside the tavern showed a blond prince in impossible armor..."
- **Defense applied:** Distributed civic texture
- **Why it survived:** The kingdom's ignorance is built from visible objects, village business, and ordinary people in motion rather than from pure exposition. The smiling washerwoman, runner, bells, baker, and children's paint additions all keep the lie socially embodied.
- **G5 check:** The village does not fail on actionability; even without named places, the square, pump, tavern board, and relay traffic make the settlement legible.

### KEEP 6 — The palace side-lane is physically usable and source-faithful
- **Category:** threshold / service architecture
- **Excerpt:** "Antonidas waited where he said he would, under a narrow service arch off the eastern court... Blue-white Kirin Tor light sank into old metal... Lye smell from the kitchens. Grease worn into stone..."
- **Defense applied:** Service-architecture specificity
- **Why it survived:** The palace margin stays in the building's working infrastructure rather than flattening into ceremonial abstraction. Service arch, lock plate, mortar, kitchen wear, and single-file door opening all keep the infiltration lane spatially real.
- **G5 check:** Capital City and Kirin Tor naming do not carry the beat by themselves; the threshold remains materially clear through arch, court, worn stone, and service-door mechanics.
