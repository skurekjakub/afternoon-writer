# Will institutional recall pull the investigation apart before the northward pursuit can begin?

Chapter 19 asks whether Silvermoon and Dalaran can still reclaim the people carrying the true answer. The answer is no, but the chapter makes that no expensive.

Vereesa brings recall, desertion law, and Lor'themar's nearly spent political cover into camp. Jaina makes the second call to Antonidas and then closes the line herself. By the time Sylvanas says north aloud, the investigation has already survived two different attempts to fold it back into institution.

This thread resolves in chapter 19, but it leaves important residue: the pursuit continues with almost no official scaffolding left. That is not triumph. It is a narrowing.
