# An unauthorized human mage crossed the Thalassian wards

## Chapter 1-2 state

As a hook, this thread resolves in Chapter 2. The breach was real, politically dangerous, and unauthorized — but it was not sabotage. It was Jaina forcing a warning across the line because she believed the line itself was already compromised.

That resolution matters because it frees the story to move on from the mere fact of trespass to the larger crisis the trespass uncovered. The breach stops being the mystery and becomes the inciting violation that got the right people onto the same road.
