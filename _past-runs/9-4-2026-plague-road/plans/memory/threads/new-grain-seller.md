# Who is the newer grain seller on the road, and does he matter?

Status: **advanced**

## Reader knows
The repeating farewell has now surfaced five times across villages, trap ground, army movement, and urban flight. Chapter 17 makes the pattern harder to file as coincidence and still impossible to explain cleanly.

## POV knows
Sylvanas now knows the phrase can surface from a frightened carter who also offers practical route intelligence, which widens the thread beyond simple roadside memory or camp coincidence.
