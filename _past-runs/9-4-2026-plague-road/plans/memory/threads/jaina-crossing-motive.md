# Why did Jaina Proudmoore cross into Quel'Thalas alone?

## Chapter 1-2 state

This thread resolves in Chapter 2. Jaina gives the answer plainly: the pattern was worsening, nobody in Dalaran was moving fast enough, Antonidas did not send her, and she left only a note before riding north for proof. The crossing was not espionage or diplomatic assignment. It was unilateral urgency.

What remains available for future use is not the factual question but the judgment question. The story now knows why she did it. It can still keep arguing about whether she was right to do it that way.
