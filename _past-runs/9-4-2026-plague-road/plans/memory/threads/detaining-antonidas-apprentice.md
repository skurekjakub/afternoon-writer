# What are the political consequences of detaining Antonidas's apprentice at the border?

## Chapter 1-2 state

This thread stays open, but Chapter 2 changes its shape. The problem is no longer simply that Sylvanas detained Antonidas's apprentice. It is that the apprentice crossed without sanction, proved useful, and was released only into controlled escort because the field emergency was too real to waste time on ceremony.

That makes the political fallout messier, not cleaner. Officially, Jaina had no authority to be there. Practically, she was right. The thread therefore remains alive for later chapters that want Dalaran, Antonidas, or Silvermoon to start asking what exactly happened at the pass and who gets to claim they acted responsibly.
