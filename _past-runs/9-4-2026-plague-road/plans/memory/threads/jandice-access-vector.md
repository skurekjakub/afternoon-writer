## Chapter 13 movement
Chapter 13 confirms that the same pride also poisons the witness account Jandice gives Sylvanas
after being shut out of the room. Jandice's pride first opens the lower route for Jaina and
then, in chapter 13, her wounded resentment opens the fast crypt stair for Sylvanas. The access-
vector question is answered twice: recognition gets the door open, grievance keeps it open after
the hierarchy wounds her.
