# Can the Andorhal cell be breached through a live capture before the district reacts?

## Chapter 8 resolution
Yes, but only briefly. The team proves the snatch can be done. They get a live operative into a controlled room and force real structure out of him. That answers the operational question chapter 7 handed forward.

The cost of the answer is the important part. The witness dies before the command layer is fully exposed, and the city's response lands quickly enough to show that success at the body level is not success at the intelligence level. The thread therefore resolves by opening harsher ones: the network rehearses compromise, Andorhal is only a throat, and the mind above the city still has to be hunted elsewhere.
