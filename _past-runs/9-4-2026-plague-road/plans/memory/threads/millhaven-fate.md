# What happened to Millhaven?

## Chapter 2-3 state

This thread begins as dread at a distance and resolves in Chapter 3 as a village-scale answer. Millhaven was not burned out, not evacuated cleanly, and not left as a battlefield. It was interrupted. People died inside ordinary household life. Later they rose. Then many of them moved north out of the village.

The answer matters because it comes from convergence, not a single clue. Domestic interiors show life cut off mid-motion. Mira confirms death and later rising. The ground behind the houses shows mass movement. The north road proves direction. By chapter end the question of what happened to Millhaven is no longer open. The village is lost.

What remains open is not fate but source. Millhaven's story is solved operationally. The vector that made it happen is not.
