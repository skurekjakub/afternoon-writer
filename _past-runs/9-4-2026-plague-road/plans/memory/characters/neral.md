# Neral

## Chapter 2-3 state

Chapter 2 introduced Neral as the strongest rider in the escort. Chapter 3 reveals what that steadiness is really for. She is good with frightened horses, yes, but more importantly she is good with fear itself. The cellar rescue works because she approaches Mira the way she approaches a panicking mount: empty hands, low voice, one small instruction at a time, no sudden claims she cannot back with tone. She does not demand trust. She makes the next step feel survivable.

That ability keeps unfolding through the chapter. She negotiates with Sylvanas for food before questions without turning the moment into challenge. She gets Mira mounted. She makes a joke about the mare biting men and never children. By the time the dispatch leaves north, Neral feels like the only possible rider to take both warning tube and survivor together. The prose has earned that assignment completely.

Write her as practical reassurance. She is not sentimental. She is efficient in a softer register than Sylvanas allows herself. Her competence is the kind that lowers panic by lowering the size of the next task.
