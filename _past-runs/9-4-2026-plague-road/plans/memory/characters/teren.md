# Teren

## Chapter 7 position
Teren is not a developed point-of-view figure in chapter 7. He is a tell. The chapter uses him as one of the first men who proves Havel’s suspicions are not paranoia and Sylvanas’s city read is landing on the right targets.

## What chapter 7 establishes
He is tall, limps, watches exits instead of loads, and carries the wrong kind of discipline for ordinary yard labor. That is enough for chapter 7: Teren exists as a visible seam in the hidden cell, and likely as one of the bodies the same-night capture plan could be built around.
