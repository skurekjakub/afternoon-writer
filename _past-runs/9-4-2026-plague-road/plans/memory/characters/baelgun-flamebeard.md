# Baelgun Flamebeard

## Overview
Also known as Baelgun. First appears in chapter22 and is current through chapter22.

## Presence on the page
Broader than Muradin and older about the eyes, with a beard clasped in iron gone white with rime and the built solidity of a dwarf who has kept a hard camp standing in killing cold.

## Voice and behavior
Speaks in blunt dwarven command compression, giving verdicts and orders in the same breath and stripping comfort out of every sentence that might slow useful work. Steps straight into Muradin’s stretcher handoff, reads faces at a glance, and keeps turning from one practical problem to the next without a wasted movement.

## What chapter 22 changes
Takes Muradin into camp treatment immediately, assigns hands without ceremony, and gives Jaina and Sylvanas only the one useful answer he can honestly promise: alive. Refuses to let the group settle into warmth or grief once he understands they carry the only workable anti-blade intelligence south. Knows Muradin reached camp alive enough to fight for and understands from Jaina’s shaking pattern-work that the cave yielded one thin piece of usable intelligence. Understands the weather window back to Daggercap Bay is short enough that waiting for rest would mean failure.

## Capabilities
Reads wounded bodies, spent soldiers, and shrinking weather windows instantly enough to turn chaos at the camp edge into disciplined triage. Commands dwarven field labor with absolute practical authority.
