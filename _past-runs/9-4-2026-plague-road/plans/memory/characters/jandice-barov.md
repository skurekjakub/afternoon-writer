# Jandice Barov

## Current read
Furious, stung, and practical enough to run the instant attention turns elsewhere.

## Chapter 13
Chapter 13 puts Jandice back on page as living scholarly volatility: Barov burgundy, reagent-
burned hands, chalk dust in the sleeve seam, and the bruised urgency of someone who is angrier
about exclusion than afraid of an intruder. Gives up the study below, the old crypt stair, and
the basic truth that Jaina was being kept there because she could keep up. Cuts herself free and
leaves before the ascent brings the others back past the cabinet.

## Voice and habits
Her chapter-13 voice is bitterness sharpened into useful detail. She tells the truth through
insult, making every answer about Jaina and Kel'Thuzad sound half like report and half like
grievance.

## What the story now knows
Knows the fast covert route to the sanctum runs down the old crypt spine past the hidden
workrooms rather than along the guest route. Also knows Jaina and Kel'Thuzad spent enough time
in real two-way exchange for jealousy to turn into a poisoned but accurate read.

## Arc position
Chapter 13 shifts Jandice from threshold guide to live loose end. Her need to be understood
still opens doors, but now it also leaves her wounded, useful, and at large.

## Chapter-end objective
Stay alive, get clear of the upper levels, and leave the sanctum damage for other people to pay
first. Preserve herself after being humiliated both by Kel'Thuzad's exclusion and by Sylvanas's
grip.
