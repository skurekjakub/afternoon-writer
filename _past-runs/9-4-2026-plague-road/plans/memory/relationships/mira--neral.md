# Mira / Neral

## Chapter 3 state

Mira and Neral form a relationship in a matter of minutes, but it is one of the chapter's clearest emotional structures. Neral is the first person who talks to Mira in terms the child can survive: look at me, one step, then another, if you fall I've got you. That sequence is why Mira comes up from the cellar at all.

After that, trust keeps showing in practical ways. Mira lets Neral feed her, mount her, wrap her in a cloak, and carry her north. Neral never plays savior. She plays steadiness. The bond is therefore immediate but believable: not deep history, not sentiment, just the hard fact that in this chapter Neral becomes the shape safety takes.
