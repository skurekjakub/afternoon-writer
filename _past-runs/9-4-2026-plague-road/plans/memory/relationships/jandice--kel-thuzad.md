# Jandice Barov / Kel'Thuzad

## Current state
Hierarchy exposed and then discarded. Jandice still measures herself by Kel'Thuzad's regard, but
chapter 13 proves he left her outside the real room the instant Jaina mattered more.

## Power dynamic
Kel'Thuzad holds the right to dismiss, exclude, and define importance. Jandice is left with
wounded status, secondhand knowledge, and the chance to survive by running first.

## Chapter 13 dynamics
Chapter 13 makes their dynamic visible through absence: Jandice is physically in the upper
levels because Kel'Thuzad sent her away from the conversation below.

## Key history
Jandice tells Sylvanas that Kel'Thuzad sent her out because Jaina could keep up and he wanted
the real exchange to himself. Her testimony proves the hierarchy had already narrowed to two
minds in the sanctum before the interruption arrived. Once the fight below claims attention,
Jandice saves herself rather than trying to retake her place in Kel'Thuzad's confidence.

## Trajectory
Delegated control -> proud subordinate at the threshold -> wounded exclusion -> loose surviving
remnant of the hierarchy.
