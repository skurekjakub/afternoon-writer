# Dagny Ironhull / Jaina Proudmoore

## Overview
This relationship begins as passage bought under pressure, but chapter 20 gives it more texture than a simple hire. Dagny and Jaina are both carrying the crossing in different ways, and the tension between those forms of competence matters.

## Chapter 20 update
Dagny owns the ship. Jaina owns the reason the crossing keeps tipping toward the unnatural. That means they are cooperating without ever becoming intimate. The relationship should be remembered as mutually necessary but not emotionally softened: a captain trying to keep a vessel alive and a mage trying to keep the larger pursuit from losing the source entirely.

## Trajectory
Chapter 20 establishes a hard transactional alliance between ship command and magical necessity.
