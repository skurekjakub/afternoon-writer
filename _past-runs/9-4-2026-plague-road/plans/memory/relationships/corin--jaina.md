# Corin / Jaina Proudmoore

## Chapter 8 state
The relationship deepens in chapter 8 by surviving the moment when most local contacts would become dead weight. Corin does not ride at the center of the escape or solve the pursuit. What he does instead is equally important for continuity: he helps make Kel'Thuzad nameable, then accepts the heavier local assignment that follows from that success.

Jaina's trust in him therefore changes shape. In chapter 7 he was the junior mage with the right papers and the right door. In chapter 8 he becomes the man she leaves behind on purpose, because the records inside Andorhal matter and he is one of the few people she trusts to keep them alive.
