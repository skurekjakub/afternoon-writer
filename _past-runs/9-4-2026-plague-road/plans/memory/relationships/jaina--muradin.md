# Jaina Proudmoore / Muradin Bronzebeard

## Overview
Chapter 21 makes them fast allies through competence rather than nostalgia.

## Chapter 21 update
Muradin brings the local truth. Jaina brings the magical frame that makes the truth worse. Their connection becomes immediate because neither wastes time pretending the other needs convincing once the evidence is on the table. By the retreat, that alliance has turned physical: Muradin is no longer just a source. He is someone Jaina helps bring back alive.
