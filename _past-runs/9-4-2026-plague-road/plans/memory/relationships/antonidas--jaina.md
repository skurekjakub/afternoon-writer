# Antonidas and Jaina Proudmoore

## Current state
Chapter 22 restores live contact under almost no usable authority. Antonidas is physically present at the palace margins and believes Jaina at once, but the mentor-apprentice bond now operates inside political failure: he can open a side door, not command the room that matters.

## Power dynamic
Antonidas regains direct presence, institutional knowledge, and enough Kirin Tor reach to soften one lock and one route. Jaina holds the actual answer and the bodily cost of using it. The old hierarchy survives emotionally, but operational authority now flows from her discovery rather than his rank.

## Physical grammar
Chapter22 finally puts them in the same physical space again: Antonidas waiting in the service-arch shadow, taking Jaina in at a glance, and shortening immediately from mentor-distance to emergency briefing distance. The reunion is all compression instead of embrace. Jaina is braced against white stone and near collapse; Antonidas listens, asks the one necessary sequence question, and uses his body to hold the corridor while the plan sharpens.

## Latest movement in the relationship
Antonidas reaches Capital City ahead of them with two Kirin Tor magi, proving that he answered Jaina’s warning as far as palace access and speed allowed. When Jaina gives the three-school sequence, Antonidas does not argue her out of it; he helps refine the timing problem and open the service lane that lets her attempt it.

## Trajectory
Mentor with institutional weight -> mentorship strained by unilateral action -> hard trust across distance -> damaged lifeline of calls and omissions -> internalized mentor standard -> chapter 22’s live reunion under crippled authority.

## Chapter 24 update
Chapter 24 turns the relationship into shared truth without shared power. Antonidas believes Jaina completely, but that belief now has to survive a public room determined to treat his loyalty as bias instead of expertise.
