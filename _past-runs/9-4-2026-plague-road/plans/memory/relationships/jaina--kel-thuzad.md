# Jaina Proudmoore / Kel'Thuzad

## Current state
Ended in lethal clarity. They reach full peer-speed understanding, then Jaina uses the room
against him and leaves carrying the answer he wanted her to hear.

## Power dynamic
Kel'Thuzad controls disclosure until combat. Jaina turns that disclosure into containment and
refuses to give him the moral victory of calling the crossing clean.

## Chapter 13 dynamics
Chapter 13 begins with the academic tableau still intact: Jaina by the boards, Kel'Thuzad behind
the desk, both bodies arranged like a lesson still in progress. The room then converts instantly
into lethal geometry once Jaina seals exits and Kel'Thuzad tries to pulse power through the
floor.

## Key history
Kel'Thuzad greets Sylvanas by praising Jaina's mind, making the room harder rather than easier
to survive. Jaina turns the sanctum into containment instead of a duel of raw force, proving she
learned everything she needed from the room except how to leave it cleanly. Kel'Thuzad falls in
the sanctum and Jaina carries the mechanism out without carrying his frame for it.

## Trajectory
Intellectual recognition -> moral duel -> lethal collaboration against him -> answer carried
away after his death.
