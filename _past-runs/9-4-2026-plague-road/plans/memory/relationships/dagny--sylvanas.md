# Dagny Ironhull / Sylvanas Windrunner

## Overview
Chapter 20 gives Dagny and Sylvanas a compact but clear command relationship. They do not become friends, and they do not need to. What matters is that each woman recognizes the other's authority inside her own domain.

## Chapter 20 update
Dagny rules the Wolfheart. Sylvanas rules her pursuit line. The crossing works because neither woman tries to collapse those spheres into one command hierarchy. Instead they cooperate at the seam between them: deck safety, pace, and the problem of getting everyone to Daggercap Bay alive.

## Trajectory
Chapter 20 establishes a clean professional respect between two women used to command, each inside a different system.
