# Arthas Menethil / Muradin Bronzebeard

## Overview
Chapter 21 reduces this relationship to its harshest useful truth: warning arrives, history speaks, and Frostmourne answers louder.

## Chapter 21 update
Muradin is the last person in the chapter who still stands close enough to Arthas to speak from the older world. That is exactly why his failure hurts. The mentor bond is real. It is simply no longer strong enough to matter against the blade.
