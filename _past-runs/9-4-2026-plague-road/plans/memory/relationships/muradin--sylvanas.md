# Muradin Bronzebeard / Sylvanas Windrunner

## Overview
This relationship is built in one chapter out of mutual recognition under pressure.

## Chapter 21 update
Muradin sees Sylvanas and understands almost immediately what sort of commander has reached his wall. Sylvanas sees Muradin and understands equally fast that he is the last useful local witness on the line to Frostmourne. That clarity lets them skip ceremony and go straight to work. The retreat then hardens that respect into obligation when Sylvanas helps carry him out alive.
