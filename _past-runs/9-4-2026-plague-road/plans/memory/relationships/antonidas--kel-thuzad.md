# Antonidas / Kel'Thuzad

## Chapter 8 state
Chapter 8 introduces this relationship by reaction rather than by flashback. Jaina says the name Kel'Thuzad. Antonidas goes silent. That silence is enough to establish that the connection is old, loaded, and institutional in a way the field team cannot fully unpack yet.

What matters for continuity is that Kel'Thuzad is not merely a new architect for Antonidas. He is an old failure returning in active form. The plague therefore carries a second layer of horror for the Kirin Tor line of the story: one of their own, or once their own, built it.

## Chapter 12 update

Chapter 12 sharpens the break between them. Antonidas was never ignorant in Kel'Thuzad's telling. He was unwilling. That distinction matters. It means the relationship is no longer just mentor who failed to stop a student or colleague who went bad. It is a split over whether sound theory has any obligation to stop at the body.

Write this as an old argument still burning hot inside newer catastrophe. Kel'Thuzad uses Antonidas as the absent measure of what he despises in Dalaran: caution, ethics, the refusal to cross from elegant proof into violation. Antonidas, by implication, remains the absent standard proving that understanding the theorem and refusing the atrocity were never opposites.
