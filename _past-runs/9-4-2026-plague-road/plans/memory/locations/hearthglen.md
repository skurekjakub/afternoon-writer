# Hearthglen

## Chapter 15 profile
Chapter 15 is the first chapter to put Hearthglen fully on the page, and the place earns its reputation immediately. It is not a village. It is a fortified Lordaeron town with walls, towers, chapel roof, smithy, stables, triage lanes, and command tents. The terrible change is that all of that infrastructure now exists to support one ugly rhythm: keep the wounded breathing, keep the dead from standing back up, keep the pyres fed.

## What to remember
Hearthglen has not won anything. It has merely held. That distinction matters. In continuity terms the town should now be remembered as a place surviving by constant reclearing, holy fire, and minutes bought at body-cost, not as a stable military refuge.
