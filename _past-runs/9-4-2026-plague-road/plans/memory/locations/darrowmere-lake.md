# Darrowmere Lake

## Current read
Low white haze over green-black water, one shoved-free boat, a green ward shimmer over the
approach, and Caer Darrow resolving out of mist in hard pieces. Wrapped oarlocks, small black
sounds of water on the hull, and a monitored quiet that feels like listening. Wet stone, mineral
cold, lake damp, and the first sterile edge of preserving work when the island draws close. Ward
pressure at the back of the teeth, slick mortar under the hands, and mud gripping the keel on
the return shore. A watched threshold. The lake turns approach, breach, and escape into one
continuous test.

## Chapter 13
Sylvanas and Cyndia cross at the east wall, using the lake itself as cover for a side-window
breach into Caer Darrow. Jaina, Sylvanas, and Cyndia make the return crossing in silence before
the southbound relay turns the lake behind them into closed ground again.

## Established facts
The outer ward over Darrowmere can be muted by hugging the east wall rather than taking the
public landing straight across. A covert crossing over Darrowmere remains viable by boat if
noise discipline and wall pressure are managed together.
