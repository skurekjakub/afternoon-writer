# Muradin's Camp

## Overview
Muradin's Camp sits in Fortified dwarven field camp inland from Daggercap Bay in Northrend. and is current through chapter22.

## Sensory profile
Sight: A snowfold yard below Muradin’s wall: hard-packed ground, two hide roofs lashed low against the wind, a forge trench breathing sparks, and stretcher hands already moving before questions finish. Sound: Forge snap, awls clicking behind hide flaps, challenge-and-answer camp rhythm, and the clipped labor of dwarves used to casualties. Smell: Coal smoke, hot metal, blood thawing in beard and leather, and the brief painful promise of warmth leaking from the forge trench. Touch: Hard-packed snow underfoot and isolated pulses of forge heat that hurt after Dragonblight cold. Atmosphere: Muradin’s camp in chapter22 is not refuge so much as a hinge: the one place bodies can be handed off before the survivors are thrown straight back onto the larger road.

## Story function
The camp preserves the final useful local intelligence about Arthas’s route, timing, and the blade waiting in the cave. Baelgun Flamebeard commands the camp’s immediate response and can promise treatment for Muradin, not strategic shelter for the rest of the formation. The camp functions as the last handoff point between Northrend casualty work and the southbound race back to Lordaeron.

## What happens here
Sylvanas’s party reaches the camp, receives Muradin’s briefing on Frostmourne, and launches the final run to the cave from there. Muradin is handed over alive enough to keep, and Baelgun sends Jaina and Sylvanas back toward Daggercap Bay before comfort can slow them.
