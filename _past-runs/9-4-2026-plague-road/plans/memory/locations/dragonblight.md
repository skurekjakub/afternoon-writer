# Dragonblight

## Overview
Dragonblight sits in Central Northrend inland corridor south of the glacier country. and is current through chapter22.

## Sensory profile
Sight: Dragon ribs humped under snow, vicious glare off white waste, and a return line marked only by old bones and the group’s own tracks back toward the bay. Sound: Powder snow hissing under boots, wind skimming the drifts, and silence big enough to make every stop feel dangerous. Smell: Dry killing cold, old bone, leather, and the wrong warm bakery note rising from Jaina even here. Touch: A cold that works through every seam and makes bread-warm skin feel obscene rather than comforting. Atmosphere: Dragonblight becomes the chapter’s thinking ground: too exposed for grief, too long for denial, and empty enough that the anti-blade framework can finally take shape there.

## Story function
Tracks through Dragonblight confirm Arthas forced a hard march inland rather than consolidating at the coast. Chapter22 confirms Dragonblight’s dragon-bone waste as the exposed route back from Baelgun’s camp to Daggercap Bay, with no cover worth slowing for. The return march through Dragonblight is where Jaina turns Frostmourne’s seam into a three-school framework.

## What happens here
The pursuit crosses Dragonblight under mounting cold and follows the inland line toward Muradin’s camp and Frostmourne. The party trudges south through dragon bones and snow glare while Jaina works out the taking-to-throwing turn inside Frostmourne’s field.

## People tied to this location
Cyndia, Jaina Proudmoore, Muradin Bronzebeard, Sylvanas Windrunner
