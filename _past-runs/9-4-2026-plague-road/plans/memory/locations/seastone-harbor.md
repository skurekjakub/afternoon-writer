# Seastone Harbor

## Overview
Seastone Harbor enters in chapter 20 as the place where the story stops being a road pursuit and becomes a sea chase. It is not treated as picturesque coastline. It is treated as a working port: damp, practical, and indifferent to the emotional stakes of the people arriving there.

## Chapter 20 update
What matters about Seastone Harbor is threshold function. Up to this point the pursuit has depended on horses, relays, camps, and inland command habits. At Seastone Harbor those habits stop being enough. The quay, the Wolfheart, and Captain Dagny Ironhull all force the protagonists into a different system where balance, weather, price, and seaworthiness matter as much as magical certainty.

Because of that, the harbor should be remembered as the last controlled edge of the old campaign. After this point the journey is colder, less supported, and much harder to regulate through any institution still behind them.
