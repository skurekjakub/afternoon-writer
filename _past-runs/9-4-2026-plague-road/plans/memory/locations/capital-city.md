# Capital City

## Overview
Capital City sits in Royal capital of Lordaeron. and is current through chapter22.

## Sensory profile
Sight: Pale walls, high towers, the palace dome taking the last of the sun, flower paste ground into cobbles, blue ribbon shreds in gutter water, and a vast banner reading WELCOME HOME, PRINCE ARTHAS above relaxed guards. Sound: Cheerful bells, wagon checks done in bored courtesy, and civic normalcy still running as if the kingdom had actually been saved. Smell: Autumn road dust, city stone, horse sweat, and the colder wrongness deepening near the palace. Touch: Soft Lordaeron damp outside the walls giving way to colder palace-adjacent stone as the group nears the inner court. Atmosphere: Capital City in chapter22 is triumph used as camouflage. The city’s public surface does more to hide Arthas than any ward or guard line.

## Story function
Arthas has been in Capital City for four days by the time the protagonists arrive, and the public story still frames him as the prince who ended the threat. Peacetime gate posture remains in effect even while Northrend guards settle into interior palace rotation.

## What happens here
The formation rides through a celebrating city, meets Antonidas at the eastern service arch, and reaches the palace margins without raising public alarm.

## People tied to this location
Antonidas, Cyndia, Jaina Proudmoore, Sylvanas Windrunner, Uther the Lightbringer

## Chapter 24 update
By chapter24 Capital City has shifted from celebration to controlled public aftermath. The city tries to metabolize the deaths in the palace through legal ritual and official story, but outside pressure is already building around that version of events. Future chapters should remember the capital as a place where public order is still functioning even while the truth underneath it is fraying fast.
