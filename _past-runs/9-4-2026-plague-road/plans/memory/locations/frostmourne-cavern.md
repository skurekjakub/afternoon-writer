# Frostmourne Cavern

## Overview
The Frostmourne cavern is chapter 21’s answer room.

## Chapter 21 update
Everything in the chapter narrows toward it: the burned harbor, the inland tracks, the camp briefing, the warning inscription, the abandoned hammer outside. Once the party crosses into the chamber, the fight stops obeying ordinary field expectations. Arrows fail. Magic slides. Muradin falls. The room teaches them that Frostmourne is not just a weapon in a place; it is the governing logic of the place itself.
