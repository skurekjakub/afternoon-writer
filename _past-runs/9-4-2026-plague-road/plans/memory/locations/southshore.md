# Southshore

## Overview
Southshore sits in Harbor town on Baradin Bay in the Hillsbrad Foothills, serving as a road hub into Lordaeron. and is current through chapter22.

## Sensory profile
Sight: Piers, gulls, slant-roofed cottages, and a calm working harbor emerging from mist after Northrend ice. Sound: Harbor noise and stable questions instead of alarm, the ordinary sounds of a town that does not know the kingdom is already lost. Smell: Wet wood, bay air, horse sweat, and softer earth after Dragonblight and open water. Touch: Warm damp replacing Northrend cold, enough to feel wrong on skin that has learned harsher climates. Atmosphere: Southshore lands as false relief: civilized, intact, and fatally unwarned, a functioning harbor town living inside the kingdom’s wrong story.

## Story function
Southshore is established in chapter22 as the first Lordaeron landfall on the return, ordinary enough to prove the warning failed in practical terms. The town serves as immediate transfer from ship to overland pursuit, with horses bought and the King’s Road taken at once.

## What happens here
The Wolfheart makes Southshore, Jaina nearly collapses on the dock, and the formation converts sea return into a reckless ride toward Capital City.

## People tied to this location
Cyndia, Dagny Ironhull, Jaina Proudmoore, Sylvanas Windrunner
