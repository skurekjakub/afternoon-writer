# Northrend

## Overview
Northrend stops being a coast in chapter 21. It becomes a campaign system.

## Chapter 21 update
The continent now has depth: the bay behind them, Dragonblight ahead, the dwarf camp as the last living hinge, and the cave as the point where every earlier fear resolves into contact. That layered geography matters because it teaches the cast the new rule of the theater. In Northrend, lateness compounds.

By chapter 21 the land itself feels aligned with obsession. Every mile inland confirms that Arthas has already spent men, ships, and time in ways the pursuit cannot reverse. Northrend is no longer merely where answers wait. It is where those answers start costing bodies.
