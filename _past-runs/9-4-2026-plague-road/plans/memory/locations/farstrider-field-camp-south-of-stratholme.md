# Farstrider field camp south of Stratholme

## Overview
Chapter 19 opens in a camp that is not meant to last. It is a recovery shape, not a home: a little order rebuilt on the south road so bodies, tack, and thought can catch up just long enough for the next choice to become possible.

What defines the place is discipline under residue. Stratholme still stains the northern sky. Ash rides the air. But the camp itself is clean in the Farstrider way: quiet horses, quiet hands, the map laid over crates, fire kept low, everything arranged so motion can begin again without drama.

## What the camp does in the story
This is the chapter's decision room. Jaina comes out with papers before food. Sylvanas reads bodies, smoke, and road. Cyndia keeps the practical lane clear. Then Vereesa rides in and turns the camp into a collision point between field truth and home authority.

Because the camp is so temporary, every conversation in it feels like a countdown. No one settles. Even the moments of stillness have departure in them. By the end of the chapter the place has done its work: it held the recall, the refusal, the severed stone call, and the first clean turn north.
