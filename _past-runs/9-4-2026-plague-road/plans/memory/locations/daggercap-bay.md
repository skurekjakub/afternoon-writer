# Daggercap Bay

## Overview
Daggercap Bay sits in Northrend coast and is current through chapter22.

## Sensory profile
Sight: Black wreckage frozen at the edge, hacked docks, ropes stiff as iron, old burn scars in the pilings, and the Wolfheart already being turned under shouted seamanship. Sound: Handspikes, curses, snapped deck orders, pigeons fighting the wicker dark, and harbor work moving too fast for relief. Smell: Tar, kelp, torn pitch, old char, and the sweeter wrongness rising from Jaina after too many days of magical strain. Touch: Harbor cold under hard white light, deck planks slick with spray, and the ship’s rail biting back into numb hands. Atmosphere: In chapter22 the bay is not a threshold into Northrend but a brutal turntable: warnings launched, ship reversed, and no emotional pause allowed between catastrophe and return.

## Story function
Dagny gives the inland party two good days, maybe three with help breaking ice, before the bay stops functioning as a sane extraction point. Daggercap Bay can still serve as an extraction and launch point only if the ship is turned immediately and nobody wastes the harbor on recovery. Every available warning lane is spent from Daggercap Bay — bird, stone, and relay logic alike — before the Wolfheart clears the ice.

## What happens here
The Wolfheart completes the crossing and the pursuit learns from the harbor state that Arthas is still far ahead inland. In daylight the pursuit reads the burned fleet, learns the return window is already collapsing, and turns inland at once. Dagny turns the Wolfheart at speed, Farstrider warnings go south by pigeon, and Jaina reopens the Antonidas line before the schooner runs back for Lordaeron.

## People tied to this location
Cyndia, Dagny Ironhull, Jaina Proudmoore, Muradin Bronzebeard, Sylvanas Windrunner
